Cources Offered

B.COM

The Bachelor of Commerce degree is designed to provide students with a wide range of managerial skills, while building competence in a particular area of business (see aside). Most universities, therefore, plan the degree such that in addition to their major, students are exposed to general business principles.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications is a three-year undergraduate degree course in computer applications. A student undergoes rigorous academic discipline to learn about database management systems, networking, data structure, and core programming languages like Python, ‘C’, and ‘Java’.

BMS

Bachelor of Management Studies is an undergraduate program for management studies offered by many universities throughout the world. Management studies programmes provide students with a solid foundation in organizational behavior and human resource management while electives in labor-management relations.

BMM

Bachelor of Mass Media is a three-year undergraduate Mass Communications programme. B.M.M. covers various subjects related to communication and its mediums like newspaper, radio.The course imparts theoretical as well as practical knowledge to the candidates in the first two years of the course and in the final year.

BSC-IT

BSc IT full form is Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. It is an undergraduate course of 3 years divided into 6 semesters. BSc IT syllabus is based on software, databases, and networking designed to impart theoretical and practical knowledge on storing, processing, and managing information securely.

BAF

A bachelor’s in Finance and Accounting is an undergraduate degree that explores subjects like Accounting, Financial Institutions, Market Systems, Banking, Management, etc. Running for a period of 3 years, its course curriculum can vary from one institution to another.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration is a bachelor's degree in business administration.In the United States, the degree is conferred after four years of full-time study in one or more areas of business concentrations. In Europe, the degree is conferred after three years of full-time study in one or more areas of business concentrations.

BE

A Bachelor of Engineering is a first professional undergraduate academic degree awarded to a student after three to five years of studying engineering at an accredited university. Professional engineering institutions as suitable for registration as an incorporated engineer or chartered engineer with further study to masters level.

BA

Bachelor of Arts (BA or AB; from the Latin baccalaureus artium, baccalaureus in artibus, or artium baccalaureus) is a bachelor's degree awarded for an undergraduate program in the arts or in some cases other disciplines. A Bachelor of Arts degree course is generally completed in three or four years, depending on the country and institution.
